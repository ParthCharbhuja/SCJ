Serving Purity Since 1985

The Timeless Shine of Trust

In the heart of Vadodara, where culture meets craftsmanship, a humble dream took root in the year 1985—a dream born not of ambition, but of devotion to quality, honesty, and service. That dream was named Shree Charbhuja Jewellers.


What began as a small silver business in a modest corner of the city has grown into a symbol of trust and excellence, cherished by generations of loyal customers. At the core of this journey stands Rajubhai, the beloved founder and guiding soul behind the brand. Known across Vadodara not just for his exceptional knowledge of silver jewellery, but for his kindness, ethics, and customer-first approach, Rajubhai has created more than a business—he has built a legacy.


With a simple motto—"To earn trust"—we have always placed people above price tags. Every ornament reflects the promise of purity, affordability, and transparency. We believe jewellery is not just an accessory; it's a feeling, a memory, a blessing, and we treat it with the respect it deserves.


Come, be a part of our story. Let us adorn your special moments with the grace and purity of silver, just like we have been doing since 1985.
